Japanese for English speakers 日本語

A series of interactive lesson packs that take an English speaker from zero Japanese toward functional fluency. Sister project to 2izzo/studio (music theory) — same format, different domain.

Each pack is four single-file HTML lessons plus an index. Click to hear a kana, type the romaji to drill it, work through a quiz at the end of each lesson. No accounts, no tracking, no localStorage — just the page and the audio.
